What Is Conversion Rate?

Conversion rate measures how many of your visitors, email recipients, or ad clickers take the action you want — signing up, buying, or contacting you. It's one of the most important metrics for Australian marketers because it tells you how well your offer and landing page are working before you spend more on traffic.

The formula is simple: Conversion Rate = (Conversions ÷ Visitors) × 100. A 3% landing page conversion rate means 3 out of every 100 visitors became a lead or customer.

Improving your conversion rate is often more cost-effective than increasing ad spend. Even moving from 2% to 4% doubles your leads without spending another dollar on traffic.
